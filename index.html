Deconstruction Phase Terms

Golden Handcuffs
High salary, stock options, or prestige that make leaving a dissatisfying job financially or psychologically difficult.
Terminal Drift
The gradual but accelerating divergence between your core values and your career path over time.
Competence Burden
The problem of being highly competent at a job you dislike, leading to promotions that make escape harder.
The Shadow Resume
A list of tasks and projects you secretly enjoy and would do for free, which often holds the key to your true vocation.

Reconstruction & Launch Terms

Eudaimonic Profit
A measure of psychological well-being, defined by Aristotle, achieved through purposeful and virtuous action.
Minimum Viable Life (MVL)
The lowest financial baseline required to comfortably support one's values and pursue purposeful work, reducing financial fear.
Skill Stacking
Combining 2-3 above-average but non-mastered skills to create a unique, high-value niche with little direct competition.
Transition Corridor
The period (typically 6-18 months) during which one maintains the old career while slowly building and validating the new vocation.
